Frequently Asked Questions

We're Here to Help

Quickly find answers to the most common questions about immigrating to Botswana.

What documents are required for a tourist visa application?
+
You will need a valid passport (minimum 6 months), a recent ID photo, proof of sufficient financial means, hotel reservation or invitation letter, and a confirmed return ticket.
How long does it take to get a work permit?
+
The standard processing time is 15 to 20 working days after receiving a complete file. Urgent applications can be processed in 7 days with additional fees. Your employer must first obtain a foreign recruitment authorization.
Can I track my application online?
+
Yes, absolutely. Log in to your personal space with your case number and you will receive real-time updates. You will also be notified by email and SMS at each important step of the process.
Which countries' citizens need a visa to enter Botswana?
+
Botswana offers visa exemption for short stays (up to 90 days) to citizens of many countries, including the EU, USA, Canada, and several African countries. Check our complete list of visa exemptions or use our eligibility verification tool.
How can I extend my visa from within Botswana?
+
You must submit an extension application at least 14 days before your current visa expires. Visit the nearest immigration office with your passport, completed application form, and applicable fees. Some extensions can be requested online.
